Our Chefs

Our Promise

At Jong-Un, we value the age-perfected traditions of our ancestors and the diversity of modern society. Our food is always prepared from recipes dating back to the Joseon Era and influenced by millenial Korean Street food. We promise to uphold tradition and celebrate diversity through our cuisine.
